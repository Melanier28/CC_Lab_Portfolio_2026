Project 5 Title

p5.js Sketch

Description

To prepare for this assignment, I explored a variety of optical illusions, ultimately focusing on still images that create the illusion of motion. I was particularly inspired by spiral patterns and decided to use this visual to mimic the movement of fan blades. I created the initial concept in Procreate and refined the design to align with my creative vision. In a real world setting, I see this piece functioning as a decorative element, bringing a sense of motion and energy to a space without the noise or mechanics of an actual fan
